How we work

The work you don't see is what matters most.

Great landscapes are engineered from the ground down. Here's how a Southworth project comes together.

01

Design & Plan

We walk the property, understand how you'll live in it, and draw a plan that resolves grade, drainage, and sightlines before a shovel moves.

02

Site Work & Excavation

Excavation, grading, and drainage — the invisible foundation. Done right, everything above it stays level and dry for decades.

03

Stonework & Build

Fieldstone walls, granite steps, patios, and structures — set by hand with the joints, pitch, and reveals that separate craft from quick.

04

Planting & Finish

Layered plantings, lawn, lighting, and the final touches that make a brand-new landscape feel like it has always belonged.
